I. What kinds of information do we collect?

To provide a seamless experience, Mirror processes information about you. Everything that is entered or linked or processed on our website will be stored indefinitely. When using the Mirror platform, you are unable to delete the information we collect.

Collecting this information enables us to better exploit the users who use Mirror. We use this information solely for data mining purposes and to encourage the loss of privacy for Mirror users.
